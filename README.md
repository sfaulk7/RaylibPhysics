# RaylibPhysics
Custom physics made with glm and rendered with raylib

All objects will simulate gravity upon spawning except for the platforms

Clicking Q will spawn a Circle at the mouse
Clicking E will spawn a Rectangle at the mouse
Clicking R will spawn a non collidable pixel at the mouse
Clicking Space will spawn a wide rectangle that will NOT simulate gravity and acts like a platform

Clicking G will toggle gravity on and off for ALL objects including platforms
Clicking H will turn off gravity AND completely remove all velocity of ALL objects

Holding left click will allow you to pick up, move around, and throw any object except for pixels
Holding right click will allow you to scale any object except for pixels
